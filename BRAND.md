# VibeScout 2026 Brand

## Brand Personality

VibeScout is sharp, cinematic, and useful. It should feel like a premium football editor made a product, not like a media company made another schedule page.

Personality words:

- Editorial
- Scout-like
- Collectible

## Voice

Short, specific, and football-literate. The voice can be dramatic when describing stakes, but it should not drift into hype copy or betting language.

Good voice examples:

- "Knockout pressure. Generational talent. This is the kind of night that rewrites legacies."
- "Worth it."
- "Chaos potential."
- "Sleep is fine."

Avoid:

- Betting odds language
- Generic sports app hype
- Corporate feature copy
- Brand-deck slogans
- Over-explaining the interface

## Logo Direction

Use the supplied VibeScout logo artwork as the canonical Step 1 logo source:

- Top lockup crop with background removed for the website header.
- Icon-only crop with background removed for app icon and compact icon moments.
- Preserve the supplied artwork as-is; do not redraw or reinterpret it unless explicitly requested.

The logo must feel like a product mark, not a tournament logo. Do not copy FIFA, ESPN, or federation marks.

## Visual Mood

The app should combine:

- Vintage World Cup poster and match-ticket energy
- Archival football photography
- Warm off-white editorial paper
- Apple-like product polish
- Green, red, and gold football accents
- Bold condensed football typography

## Anti-Patterns

Avoid:

- Generic SaaS dashboard
- Dark neon crypto look
- Betting app feel
- Sofascore or Flashscore clone
- ESPN or FIFA copycat
- Fake messy retro
- Cluttered sports-data wall
- Brand showcase sections
- Decorative animation without product value
